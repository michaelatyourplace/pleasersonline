Bachelorettes ideas in Charleston SC

Charleston bachelorette on the horizon? Get ready for Southern charm and good times! Think adorable stays downtown or breezy beach houses. Days could be filled with ' exploring history, hitting the water for a boat cruise, getting creative with a candle-making class, or treating yourselves to a spa day. Evenings are for rooftop drinks with amazing views, delicious Southern dinners, and maybe some live music. Charleston's brunch scene is legendary, so that's a must-do!

Daytime Activities

  • Walking Tour: Explore the beautiful historic streets, admire the architecture, and learn about Charleston's rich past.
  • Carriage Tour: A relaxing way to see the historic district.
  • Rainbow Row Photoshoot: Capture some iconic group photos in front of the colorful houses.
  • Charleston City Market: Browse local crafts and souvenirs.
  • Plantation Visit: Take a trip to Magnolia Plantation or Middleton Place for a glimpse into the region's history and stunning gardens.

Water Adventures

  • Harbor Cruise:Enjoy the sights from the water, perhaps on a sunset or dolphin-watching tour. Some even offer a BYOB option for a party atmosphere.
  • Private Sailing Charter:A luxurious way to see the harbor.
  • Party Boat:Several options offer a lively experience with music and dancing on the water. Consider Tiki Tours of Charleston or Saltwater Cycle.
  • Beach Day:Head to Folly Beach, Sullivan's Island, or Isle of Palms for sun, sand, and waves. You can rent paddleboards or take a yoga class on the beach.

Creative & Unique Experience

  • Candle Making Class: Create custom scents at Candlefish or Charleston Candle Co.
  • Fragrance Workshop: Design a signature perfume at Tijon Fragrance Lab.
  • Art Class: Try a painting, pottery, or glass-blowing class.
  • Jewelry Workshop: Design your own necklace or earrings at Little Bird.
  • Cooking Class: Learn to make Lowcountry cuisine with a local chef.
  • Private Yoga Class: Start the day with a relaxing session at the beach, in a park, or at your accommodation with Serenity Tree Yoga or Pushups and Prosecco.
  • Afternoon Tea: Enjoy a sophisticated experience at The Loutrel's rooftop.
  • King Street Shopping: Explore boutiques and designer stores.
  • Spa Day: Indulge in treatments at The Wentworth Mansion, Dewberry, or The Sanctuary on Kiawah Island.

Night Time Fun

  • Rooftop Bars: Charleston has many great rooftop spots with fantastic views. Check out Stars Rooftop Bar, The Citrus Club, Uptown Social, The Rooftop at the Vendue, or Élevé.
  • Cocktail Crawl: Explore the bars on Upper King Street.
  • Dinner with Southern Charm: Enjoy Lowcountry cuisine at Husk, Poogan's Porch, FIG, or The Ordinary. For seafood, try The Darling Oyster Bar or 167 Raw.
  • Live Music: Many venues around the city offer live music.
  • Dancing: Head to Republic Garden & Lounge, Trio, or Deco for a night of dancing.
  • Ghost Tour: For a spooky and historical evening activity.
